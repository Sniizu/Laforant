WELCOME TO LAFORANT!

Attention, Agent! Your time to shine has finally come. So grab your weaponry, set your sights, and put your hands up. It's time to jump into LAFORANT first. Can't leave you unprepared, now can't it? Oh, and if you want to stay up to date with LAFORANT, and its upcoming updates, I recommend keeping an eye on the main site.

WHAT ARE THE REQUIREMENTS TO PLAY IN LAFORANT COMPETITIVE MODE?

We cannot send Green Agents into battle without preparation. You must win 10 unranked matches before gaining access to Competitive Mode. If you have won 10 unrated then you better get into the game to test your mettle. Please note that if you achieved victory without your 10th rank in the previous Episode, you do not have to go through all the steps again.

WHAT ARE THE LAFORANT RANKS?

The ranks are: Iron, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Immortal, and Radiant. With the exception of Radiant and Immortal, each rank has three tiers. The higher the number, the better your rank. Your rank will play directly into your Act Rank over time (more on that later).

WHAT IS CHARACTER CONTRACT?

When it comes to dangerous missions, more is always better. More agents means more options to tackle any challenge (and any style of play). To get started, you will have five agents at your disposal. You can also recruit other agents for free, just to play the game. Search for your newest recruit as you complete the Welcome Contract to LAFORANT. As you play with those agents, you'll be able to unlock their tiers as you go, which contain everything from skins to Radianite. There are a total of 10 tiers per agent, and while you can buy Levels 1-5 with VP, Levels 6 and more require XP. Mastering the agents means you will get all the cool swag associated with them. Want to unlock another agent? After using your free recruits, simply upgrade your Agent Contract to Level 5 to recruit them.
